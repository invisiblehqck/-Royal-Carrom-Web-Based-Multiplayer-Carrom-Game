Carrom Rules (Simplified)

  • Players take turns striking. Player 1 is White, Player 2 is Black.
  • Pot your colored coins. White pots white, Black pots black.
  • The Queen (pink) can be pocketed after pocketing at least one of your own coins.
  • To secure the Queen, you must pocket one of your own coins in the *same* or *next* valid stroke.
  • If Queen is pocketed but not covered, it's returned to the center.
  • Pocketing the striker is a foul: opponent gets a turn, one of your pocketed coins is returned.
  • Pocketing opponent's coin: opponent gets a turn (unless own coin also pocketed).
  • Player with most points (or who clears their coins + Queen) wins. (Typically 29 points max or per board agreement).
  • White: 1 point per coin. Black: 1 point per coin. Queen: 3 points (if covered).
